FAQ

Common Questions

Can I upload Zillow leads? +
Yes. Zorvo AI supports lead imports from Zillow, Realtor.com, Facebook Ads, Google Ads, CSV files, CRM exports, and direct website forms. Setup takes minutes.
Can AI transfer leads to me directly? +
Yes. When a hot lead is identified and wants to speak with someone, AI can transfer the call directly to your phone in real time. If you're unavailable, the system sends a full lead summary, creates a follow-up task, and sends the lead a booking link.
What happens if I miss a transfer? +
If you can't answer, Zorvo AI handles it gracefully. The AI politely informs the lead, sends you a complete call summary, creates an automated follow-up task, and delivers property information and a booking link to the lead — no opportunity is lost.
Can AI explain property details? +
Absolutely. Zorvo AI is trained to explain property features, bedrooms, bathrooms, location benefits, nearby schools, transportation options, shopping centers, and community amenities during every call.
Can I use my own phone number? +
This is covered during your setup and demo session. Our team will walk you through number configuration options that work best for your brokerage and market.
How long does setup take? +
Most agents are up and running within 24–48 hours. Our team handles the configuration, onboarding, and first campaign setup for you. Book a demo and we'll walk you through everything.
What happens after the 30-day pilot? +
After your pilot, you'll have a clear picture of how many leads Zorvo AI converted, saved, and followed up on. You can then move to a custom subscription plan built around your lead volume and goals. No automatic charges, no surprises.
